CARPENTER
Works with wood to construct, install, and repair buildings, furniture, and other structures. Their job duties typically include:
1.Reading and interpreting blueprints, drawings, and specifications.
2.Measuring, cutting, and shaping wood and other materials using hand and power tools.
3.Maintaining and caring for carpentry tools and equipment.
Carpenters may specialize in a particular type of carpentry, such as residential or commercial construction, cabinet making, or furniture building. They may work independently or as part of a construction team, and their work may involve both indoor and outdoor settings.

Applies paint and other decorative finishes to various surfaces, including walls, ceilings, floors, furniture, and other objects. The job duties of a painter may include: 1.Preparing surfaces by cleaning, sanding, and priming them before painting. Mixing and matching paint colors to achieve the desired color or finish. Applying paint or other coatings using brushes, rollers, or sprayers. Painters may work on new construction projects, or they may be hired to repaint or refresh existing buildings, rooms, or structures.

Mason & Plumber

Masons may work on new construction projects, or they may be hired to repair or renovate existing buildings. They may work independently as contractors, or they may be employed by construction firms.
The job of a plumber is to install, repair, and maintain pipes and plumbing systems that are used for water supply, heating, and sanitation.

Electrician

Work on new construction projects, or they may be hired to repair or upgrade existing electrical systems.
